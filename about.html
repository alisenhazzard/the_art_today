About

Fine Art and Furniture by Dana Kellar

With a strong background in mechanical drafting, I have spent most of my life working in the engineering field while maintaining a strong interest in the arts. After retiring from the field of engineering, I obtained my Alternative Teaching degree in Visual Arts.

A substitute teacher by day, my passion remains creating art. I enjoy drawing and painting custom pieces of various landmarks and homes. My main interest is with landscape drawings, and I'm also fond of ocean scenes and rustic farm settings. I specialize in ink and watercolors.

In the last few years I have branched out into woodworking, focusing mainly on furniture. I've crafted dollhouses for my grand-daughter, coffee tables, shelving, butcher blocks, wedding decor, and anything else that comes to mind.

Any questions or requests for custom pieces are always welcome. Feel free to contact me by email or phone, and please see my Custom Art page for more information about custom work.


Enjoy The Art Today,
